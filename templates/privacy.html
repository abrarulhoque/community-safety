Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address when you register for an account, report an incident, or register a camera.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, to process incident reports, to communicate with you, and to help verify camera registrations.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only with verified community safety leaders and law enforcement officials as necessary to fulfill the purpose of our services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Camera Registry Information

When you register a camera, your camera's location and coverage area may be displayed on community maps, but your identity will not be visible to other community members.

5. Incident Reports

Information from incident reports may be aggregated and displayed on community maps and dashboards, but your identity will be protected unless you explicitly choose to make it public.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Account Settings

You can update your account information and privacy settings at any time through your user profile.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through a notice on our platform.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@communitysafety.org.
